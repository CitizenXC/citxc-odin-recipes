The White Lady

Description

Ingredients

  • 45ml London Dry gin
  • 20ml Triple Sec
  • 20ml lemon juice
  • 15ml simple syrup
  • 1/2 egg white

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients into shaker
  2. Dry shake
  3. Add ice and shake
  4. Double strain into chilled coupe
  5. Garnish with twist of lemon
