Royal Hawaiian

Description

The Royal Hawaiian was the signature cocktail at the well known Waikiki resort of the same name, the Royal Hawaiian Hotel. It's a tiki-esque cocktail with the unusual inclusion of gin. First created in the 1920s and it was originally name Princess Kaiulani. It was in the 1950s when it was renamed to the hotel's namesake cocktail.

The hotel has since changed it's signature cocktail to the better known Mai Tai.

Ingredients

  • 45ml gin
  • 22.5ml orgeat
  • 30ml pineapple juice
  • 15ml lemon juice

Steps

  1. Combine all the ingredients into your cocktail shaker
  2. Shake with ice
  3. Double strain into a chilled coupe glass
  4. Garnish with pineapple wedge and/or spears
